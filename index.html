Why is Rock Werchter 2020 taking place in a backyard in Obdam?

The National Security Council of Belgium has decided that large gatherings including festivals are banned until 31 August due to the coronavirus.
This is the reason for this one time shift of Rock Werchter to The Netherlands. We have searched far and wide for the right location.
We are glad to announce that the new location is the backyard of JdeB in Obdam. Everything that makes RW great will be present at festivalpark Obdam.
Such as but not limited to, music, beer, grass, tents, spring rolls, mud, sun, heat and more beer!
